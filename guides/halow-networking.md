# Wi-Fi HaLow Networking

The simplest way to get started with Wi-Fi HaLow is with a HaLowLink router.

> Wi-Fi HaLow is not available in all regions. Please check your local emission regulations before starting a HaLow network.

They can be purchased from Mouser:
- [HaLowLink 1](https://www.mouser.com/new/morse-micro/morse-micro-halowlink-1-router/)
- [HaLowLink 2](https://www.mouser.com/new/morse-micro/morse-micro-halowlink-2-gateways/)

Quick setup guides can be found [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ISVt7QTAP_0).

To ensure that HaLow traffic from the endpoints is forwarded to the
Snapserver on the 2.4 GHz or LAN network, it is simplest to set the
HaLowLink to bridge mode.
