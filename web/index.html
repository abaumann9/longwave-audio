How to flash

  1. Connect your XIAO ESP32-S3 Plus to your computer with a USB-C cable.
  2. Put the board into download mode: hold BOOT, tap RESET, then release BOOT. The board should appear as a serial port in Chrome.
  3. Select your board and region above, then click Install Firmware.
  4. Select the serial port when prompted and wait for the flash to complete.
  5. Once flashing is done, press RESET (without holding BOOT) to boot into the new firmware.

Provision with ESP SoftAP

After flashing, the endpoint advertises a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network for one-time setup. Use Espressif's SoftAP Provisioning app (not the QR / BLE variant) to push your Wi-Fi HaLow credentials.

  1. Install the app: iOS · Android.
  2. On your phone, join the device's 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network — SSID begins with PROV_, no password.
  3. Open the app, tap Provision New Device, and choose the option to connect manually (skip the QR code).
  4. Enter the proof of possession: longwave
  5. Select your Wi-Fi HaLow SSID, enter the password, and confirm.
  6. The endpoint reboots, joins the HaLow network, and connects to Snapserver.

The app may report "failed to associate" at the end — safe to ignore. The endpoint tears down its SoftAP once it has the credentials, which disconnects the phone. Provisioning succeeded.

To clear stored credentials and re-provision, hold RESET for 5 seconds until the LED flashes red.
